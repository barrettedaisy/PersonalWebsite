Ultrasonic Self-Driving Car (Advanced Undergraduate Project)

The capstone project for my undergraduate degree at Queen’s University was a self driving car that I designed and implemented myself, using Raspberry Pi and Arduino hardware. The driving decisions are made by a machine learning program written in Python.
